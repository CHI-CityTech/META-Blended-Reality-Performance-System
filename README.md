# Blended Reality Performance System (BRPS)

*A CHI / Balanced Blended Space Initiative*

## Overview

The **Blended Reality Performance System (BRPS)** is the applied testbed for the **Balanced Blended Space (BBS)** framework. BBS is a universal theoretical model for describing all communication and interaction pathways across **physical, virtual, and conceptual spaces**, and across **cognitive and computational intelligences**.

BRPS puts these principles into practice in performance contexts—such as theatre, dance, immersive installations, and interactive media—where live physical and virtual elements must interact symmetrically and in real time. It is designed as a **modular, scalable environment** that allows researchers, performers, and AI systems to co-create within a blended space.

## 1. Connection to Balanced Blended Space (BBS)

BBS proposes that all intelligences (cognitive and computational) and all spaces (physical, virtual, conceptual) should interact **symmetrically**, with no entity functioning merely as a tool. Instead, each contributes meaningfully to a shared blended reality.

BRPS is the **performance implementation** of these principles and provides:

* A real-time experimental environment for **BBS syntax testing**
* A practical space for exploring **cross-reality mediation pathways**
* A controlled domain for developing **collaborative AI systems**
* A risk-tolerant sandbox prior to applying BBS in education, medicine, or industry

## 2. Core Principles of BRPS

### 2.1 Real-Time Synchronization

BRPS synchronizes:

* Human actors, dancers, musicians
* Physical puppets, set pieces, kinetic sculptures
* Virtual avatars, digital puppets, projections
* AI-driven agents operating in virtual or physical space

Using sensors, motion capture, cameras, robotic control, and AI models, BRPS ensures **continuous bidirectional alignment** between domains.

### 2.2 AI as a Collaborative Partner

Aligned with BBS’s cognitive/computational symmetry, the AI systems in BRPS may:

* Control virtual or physical puppets
* Generate narrative or performative responses
* Adapt performances to audience behavior
* Participate in improvisational structures
* Make decisions based on environmental data

### 2.3 Cross-Reality Interaction

BRPS supports seamless interaction between:

* Virtual characters and physical sets
* AR layers and real performers
* Physical puppets whose behaviors are extended virtually
* Hybrid ensembles (human + AI musicians, actors, puppeteers)

### 2.4 Modularity and Scalability

The system is designed so that components may be assembled, removed, or rearranged to support:

* Low-tech environments (e.g., shadow puppetry)
* High-tech productions (VR/AR, robotics, AI-driven performers)
* Distributed or remote performance ensembles
* Small labs, medium stages, or large venues

### 2.5 Multi-Sensory Integration

BRPS incorporates the symmetrical sensory model defined in BBS—each sensory pathway is treated in parallel. This includes integration of:

* Visual (projection, virtual cinematography, AR overlays)
* Auditory (spatial audio, live + virtual orchestra)
* Haptic (physical puppets, actuated surfaces, tactile feedback)
* Conceptual (symbolic and narrative transformations)

### 2.6 Audience Interaction

Audience members (both physical and virtual):

* Influence narrative branches
* Interact through gestures, motion, or devices
* Affect AI-driven characters
* Participate in distributed or remote configurations

### 2.7 Seamless Spatial Transitioning

Performers, objects, and audiences may move fluidly between:

* Physical → virtual
* Virtual → physical
* Conceptual → performative manifestations

## 3. Example Applications

## 5. How BRPS Fits into the BBS Framework

BRPS is the **operational implementation** of BBS—its purpose is to:

* Validate BBS’s theoretical symmetries
* Demonstrate real-world mediation pathways
* Provide observable Δstate transitions for BBS syntax
* Explore collaborative AI through performance
* Stress-test cross-reality integration
* Serve as a universal template for future BBS-aligned systems

## 6. Summary

The **Blended Reality Performance System (BRPS)** provides a modular, extensible platform for exploring the future of performance in combinative reality. It realizes the key goals of the **Balanced Blended Space** framework by enabling symmetric interactions between cognitive and computational intelligences across physical, virtual, and conceptual domains.

BRPS supports:

* Real-time hybrid performance
* Distributed ensembles
* Collaborative AI systems
* Multi-sensory integration
* Cross-reality narrative forms
* Audience-driven emergent behavior

Ultimately, BRPS demonstrates how AI and human performers can **co-create** within a unified blended environment—advancing both artistic practice and foundational research in communicative symmetries.
